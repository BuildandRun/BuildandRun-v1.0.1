How to Access the Panel?
Step 1
You will receive a username and password from your service provider ("Build and Run") so that you can Access/Login to the Admin panel through the portal.
After getting your portal login credentials, Click here or https://brstore.us/admin to go to the admin page.
Your browser will now open our admin panel's home page. Clicking the "Access Now" or "Log in" buttons in the top right corner will take you to the Login Portal, where you can access and log into the portal.
